Pacific Islands

In the island of the Pacific, specifically in Samoa, there is a cultural tradition of having powerful meaning tattoos. This depiction of Christ is referred to as a sogaimiti. Which is a Samoan man who has a pe'a tattoo. The tattoo is applied from the waist to the knees. The word "sogaimiti" is used to describe a man who is:

Brave, Fearless, Wise, Knowledgeable, A provider, A protector.

Christ is all of these things for this Samoan artist, who depicts Christ in a light that He really did die for each and every single one of us. The reflection and respect of His culture implemented in this piece shows the love of Christ for all of those He saved.
